Frequently Asked Questions

Have Any Questions?

Listing your bike on WheelWise is simple. Just follow these steps:
  1. Click on the "Sell Now" tab in the navigation bar.
  2. Fill in the details about your bike, including make, model, year, condition, and any additional information.
  3. Upload clear photos of your bike from different angles.
  4. Set your desired price and submit the listing.
Once your listing is approved, it will be visible to potential buyers. If someone is interested, they can send you an enquiry through the "Enquiries" dropdown in the "Messages" section. You can then chat with the interested buyer and exchange details to facilitate the sale.

Updating Bike Details: On the detail page of your listed bike, you'll find an option to "Update Details". Click on it, and you can modify any necessary information, such as the bike's condition, price, or description. Don't forget to save your changes!

Deleting a Bike Listing: If you no longer wish to sell your bike, you can delete the listing from the detail page by clicking the "Delete Listing" button.

Wishlisting Bikes: While browsing bikes in the shop section or on individual bike detail pages, you'll find a "Wishlist" button. Click on it to add the bike to your wishlist. You can access your wishlist from your account dashboard and keep track of bikes you're interested in.

To send an inquiry for a bike, follow these steps:
  1. Browse the bikes available in the shop section.
  2. Click on the bike you're interested in to view its details.
  3. Below the bike details, you'll find an "Inquiry" button. Click on it.
  4. A dropdown menu will appear, allowing you to select the bike you want to inquire about.
  5. Fill in the required information in the inquiry form, such as your name, email, and message.
  6. Click on the "Send Inquiry" button to submit your inquiry.
Once you've sent the inquiry, the seller will receive your message and respond to you accordingly.
